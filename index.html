How to File a Case

Follow these simple steps to report an incident

1

Login to Your Account

Access the system using your credentials. If you don't have an account, register first.

2

Navigate to Reports

Click on the "Reports" menu from the sidebar or dashboard.

3

Fill Out the Form

Provide incident details including type, location, date, and description.

4

Upload Evidence

Attach photos, videos, or documents related to the incident (optional).

5

Submit & Track

Submit your report and receive a case number. Track progress in real-time.
